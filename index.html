    About Us

     WHO WE ARE

    TANZANIA YOUTH ACTION NETWORK (TYAN) is a student led organisation dedicated to empowering high school students by connecting them volunteering opportunities in their field of interest.


     MISSION

    To empower high school students by providing hands on volunteering opportunities in industries related to their future careers.


     VISION

    A future where every Tanzanian student gains real world experience before entering university or the workforce, helping them make informed career choices.

    Activities

    Sustainable development goals we work to achieve.

    Volunteering Opportunities

    Our network links students with relevant volunteering opportunities in their fields of interest, enabling them to gain hands-on experience, develop essential skills, and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

    Skills Development

    Our initiative fosters leadership, teamwork, and professional skills among students, equipping them with real-world experience and preparing them for future career success.

    Culture of Service and Growth

    We foster a culture of community service and career exploration, empowering students to make a meaningful impact while gaining valuable insights into their future professions.


    What Drives Us

    TANAZANIA YOUTH ACTION NETWORK is created and run by students, for students. We believe that early career exposure is key to success
