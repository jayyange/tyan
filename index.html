    About Us

     WHO WE ARE

    KAYO is a Non-Government Organization that deals with empowering community on entrepreneurship and production techniques, promote district development, community understandings in social development activities, environmental conservations, etc.


     MISSION

    To become an outstanding organization committed to empowering youth and fostering a culture of innovation, collaboration, health, gender equality and environmental conservation.


     VISION

    Our vision is a world where all youth have the opportunity to reach their full potential and contribute positively to their communities.

    Activities

    Sustainable development goals we work to achieve.

    Empowering Communities

    KAYO is collaborating with communities to provide training and support for entrepreneurship and production techniques, fostering the establishment of business and skills development centers.

    Sustainable Economic Development

    Enhance district development by improving investment opportunities in key sectors such as agriculture, trade, and small-scale industries.

    Social Development and Awareness

    Promote awareness and active community participation in social development programs, including education, health, and cultural initiatives.


    Environmental Conservation and Tourism Promotion

    Support environmental protection efforts and promote eco-tourism within local districts to ensure sustainable use of natural resources.

    Youth Empowerment and Self-Reliance

    Build capacities for youth and vulnerable groups to achieve economic independence through skills development and employment opportunities.

    Advocacy and Policy Engagement

    Strengthen youth involvement in national policy-making and governance, ensuring accountability and representation in decision-making processes.
